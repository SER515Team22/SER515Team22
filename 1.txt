Hi everyone this is team 22 presentation, for our software YoMath.

Our app focus on teaching mathematics in a fun and interactive way. Our target audience is students of standard 1 and standard 4, and faculty to aid in learning.

For a person using portal for the first time user can register on the portal.

Once registered the user can come back to login.

Based on whether a student or faculty logs in user will be redirected to the required portal.

As a faculty I can post assignments for student and view the submissions done by students.

For standard 1 we have provided simple numeric comparison.

For standard 6 we have provided playground with all basic aritmetic.
